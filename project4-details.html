Automated Console Validation Script for Standalone Physical Servers

Challenge:

  • After server reboots, several standalone physical servers (Cisco, HP, Dell) occasionally failed to come online, also we face console access issues. This delayed recovery efforts, as teams first had to resolve console connectivity before bringing the servers back online. Additionally, manual validation prior to patching was time-consuming—taking approximately 5 minutes per server.

Solution:

  • To address this, Created a automated script to validate console accessibility and login credentials for standalone physical servers (Cisco, HP, Dell).
  • Reduced manual validation time by 90% and improved post-reboot recovery by proactively identifying access issues.
  • Integrated weekly email reporting to highlight console failures and credential mismatches for timely resolution.
